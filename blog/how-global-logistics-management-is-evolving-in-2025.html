A World Economic Forum report highlights that supply chains are facing more disruption today than any time in the past 50 years. Shipping bottlenecks, labor shortages, and unpredictable fuel prices are forcing companies to rethink how they manage global logistics.

That's where GEOGREAT Supply Chain Consultancy steps in. We don't just move products — we move businesses forward.

2025's Global Logistics Challenges

  • Red Sea Shipping Disruptions: With attacks on shipping routes, global shipping rates have soared by over 300% in some regions (source).
  • Technology Demands: Smart tracking, predictive analytics, and automated warehouses are no longer a luxury. They are mandatory to stay competitive.
  • Sustainability Pressures: Companies are being pushed to reduce their carbon footprints, rethink packaging, and choose greener transport modes.

Without strong logistics strategies, businesses risk losing customers, facing delays, and paying huge unexpected costs.
