Project Procurement & Supply Chain Execution

End-to-end procurement services to ensure that projects are delivered on time and within budget.

What We Offer

Comprehensive end-to-end procurement services to ensure your projects are delivered on time, within budget, and in full compliance with regulatory requirements. Our team supports you throughout the entire procurement lifecycle from budgeting and supplier management to logistics coordination and performance tracking.

Our project procurement services include :

  • Procurement budgeting and planning
  • Document management systems for streamlined record-keeping
  • Standard operating procedures
  • Workshops on Industry Best Practices
  • Regulatory compliance support (Licensing, Permits, Certifications)
  • Import/Export compliance across sea, land, and air freight
  • Liaison with destination country regulatory and government agencies
  • Logistics risk assessments and mitigation strategies
  • Supplier and performance management

Undertaking a turnkey project?

We can manage your entire supply chain execution including sourcing (both local and offshore), production or assembly, and logistics with efficiency and precision.

Our turnkey supply chain services cover :
  • Supplier identification, negotiation, and contracting
  • Pre-shipment coordination and quality assurance
  • Shipping and freight management
  • Customs clearance
  • Final delivery to the project site
