FAQs

What is supply chain management (SCM)

SCM refers to the coordination and oversight of all activities involved in sourcing, production, logistics, and delivery of goods and services.

Why is supply chain optimization important?

It improves efficiency, reduces costs, minimizes delays, and enhances customer satisfaction.

What types of companies benefit from supply chain consulting?

Any company that handles inventory, manufacturing, distribution, or logistics can benefit from startups to large enterprises.
